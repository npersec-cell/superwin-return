How to Play

  1. Login to your account.
  2. Claim free coins when the claim timer is ready.
  3. Select one question before it closes.
  4. Choose an answer and the number of coins to use.
  5. Click Predict. Your prediction will stay in Running until the result is announced.

Reward

  • Monthly Top 10 is based on monthly performance.
  • Coin balance is separate from ranking.
  • The monthly winner receives a reward.
  • Reward proof will be shown after it is completed.

Question Time

  • Each question has its own closing countdown.
  • When a question closes, it disappears from the open list.
  • Submitted predictions stay in Running while waiting for the result.
